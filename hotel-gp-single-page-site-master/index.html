services

Food Service/ Food Runner

In our bid to deliver authentic, undiluted experiences, we’ve introduced a unique regional home style dining experience. Always prepared with a local touch, the home-style cooking ensures that you maintain good health throughout your stay. And at the same time it gives you the opportunity to dabble in the local delicacies.

Refreshment

These foods usually include foods with a lot of water. Some examples of refreshments food include juices (non-alcoholic or alcoholic), hot drinks (coffee, tea, hot chocolate, horchata), carbonated beverages, alcoholic beverages, sports drinks, dairy products, and fruit drinks.

Housekeeping

It includes food and beverage preparation and service for restaurant, lounge, coffee shops, bars, parties, and room service. It includes parking and door attendants, drivers, porters, and bell attendants. Housekeeping includes the duties of keeping the areas of the hotel clean, tidy, hygienic, and pleasant.

Room Security

Safety and security of our guests, employees and assets is paramount to us. Our security department has invested in updated technology and equipment for all our recent hotels and evaluates ways to upgrade security measures in our older hotels.
